Ongoing Research

Zero-shot emotion detection on Romanized Bengali using pre-trained LLMs

In Progress
Started: September 2025
Documentation: Research Documentation

Nusaiba Ahmed, Dipita Tasnim

Emotion recognition models have advanced rapidly with large pre-trained language models, especially in English. However, when it comes to low-resource or code-mixed dialects like Banglish (transliterated Bengali), these models often fail to capture emotional nuance due to limited training data and linguistic diversity. This work explores how lightweight pre-trained models and multilingual architectures can be adapted to recognize emotions in Banglish without extensive fine-tuning.

NLP Zero-shot Learning Few-shot Learning Hugging Face
